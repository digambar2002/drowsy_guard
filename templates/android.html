Flask App Demo

Follow these steps to access your Flask app on your mobile device:

Step 1:

Make sure your mobile device is connected to the same network as your computer running the Flask app.

Step 2:

Find the local IP address of your computer:

ipconfig

Step 3:

Run your Flask app with the following command:

flask run --host=0.0.0.0

Step 4:

Open your mobile browser and enter the following address:

http://your-computer-ip:5000

Your Flask app should now be accessible on your mobile device!
